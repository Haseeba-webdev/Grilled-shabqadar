About Us

Introduction of
The Grilled Shabqadar

The Grilled Shabqadar is Shabqadar's favourite destination for authentic Peshawari grills and traditional KPK cuisine. We cook everything over real coal flame, using locally sourced fresh ingredients and time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.
